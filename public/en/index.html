About me

I'm a Front-End web developer that loves to create engaging user interfaces. I mostly develop for the web, since it's my place of comfort, but I've also developed terminal applications and all sorts of scripts (tiny programs, usually no more than a file) to sort stuff in my computer.
I'm always on the lookout for new knowledge I can absorb and apply to my applications, and I'm a fast learner. I'm good at teamwork and I do enjoy the company of others while I'm coding, but I often work alone. I work under pressure and will get any app shipped before the due date.
